Our past, present and future

About us

Woodhaven Custom Cabinets is a veteran owned, family business right here in Northwest Arkansas. Combined, they have more than twenty years of experience in custom cabinetry and woodworking.

Our backstory

Woodhaven was started by brothers Kalvin and Kevin Cackler in 2017. For the first couple of months, they worked out of their two garages, building doors in one and cabinet boxes in the other.

Despite the tight quarters, their high quality work spoke for itself, and it wasn’t long before they were able to move into a proper woodshop.

Over the past two years, they have continued to expand, acquiring more space, more specialty tools, and more employees to get the jobs done right.

What are we now

Today, we are proud to show you what hard work, perseverance, and dedication can produce.

We have built custom kitchens and bathroom cabinets. We have made built ins that fit like puzzle pieces with stone fireplaces. We have even built high-end executive desks (that one we’re particularly proud of).

What the future holds for us

We’re always looking for the next challenge, and we’re excited to get started on our next project!
